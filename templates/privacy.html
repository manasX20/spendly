Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic technical information such as browser type and session activity, used to keep the app secure.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To identify and fix technical issues.

We do not use your financial data to serve you ads, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard safeguards including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls limited to authorized personnel only.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with or transmit your data to third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Any future integrations will be disclosed in an updated version of this policy before they go live.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, use the account settings page or contact us directly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
